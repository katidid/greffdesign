Simplicity Consulting Brand

2019
Role: Graphic Designer

Simplicity is a female founded and run consulting firm that specializes in providing flexible work opportunities for professionals looking to balance work and personal life.

I worked with the CEO and Marketing Director to freshen up the brand. After pitching a few ideas, we settled on a direction where the old brand and colors were revised rather then replaced. I made minor adjustments to the logo, cleaning up the kerning and sizing of the workmark to be more legible. I chose a color palette that is more current and easier to standardize across print and digital media. Then I organized their brand assets, created a guidelines document, and applied the new brand across a number of assets including digital documents, websites, magazine ads, and printed collateral.
