Worktank Brand

2006

During the first few months at Worktank I was tasked with contributing to the update of the companies brand and identity. I participated in the initial team brainstorms, worked with another designer on the team to design the new final logo, and created the new identity including imagery, color palette and brand standards that would be executed across all printed and online media.
