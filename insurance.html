Role: Art Director

Asenno is a start up insurance agency focusing on providing insurance for small businesses. We collaborated closely with the development team to pioneer a workflow that suited responsive web best practices. We also iterated on our creative ideas and found cost saving solutions for the clients that still upheld our team's design vision.
