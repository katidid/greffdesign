Nordstrom Mobile Website

Spring 2011
Role: Web designer

I worked closely with the front-end developers to make sure every aspect of my design was efficient enough to keep the load times down on slow mobile browsers, and worked closely with the brand team at Nordstrom to ensure that the look and feel of the site fit within the guidelines if their branded online experience.
