Alessandra Rose - You Are Gold

Summer 2012

I was asked by Alessandra Rose a local musician, singer and songwriter to help design the website, album cover, and screen-print poster for the release of her album “You Are Gold.” I brought her vision to life for the album and promotional pieces, and we worked together to do a run of screenprint posters at the Vera Project screen print studio.
