Homestead Land Trust

Summer 2010

In the spring and summer of 2010 I volunteered for an organization called Taproot. I worked with a small team of volunteers to help a local non-profit organization re-haul their website to better fit their needs. I worked on the user experience, brand and final visual design for the site. My process started with consulting with the client over the site map, creating and revising a wireframe document based on client feedback, creating a brand style guide, and doing final mockups of key webpages that the web developer could translate to the whole site.
